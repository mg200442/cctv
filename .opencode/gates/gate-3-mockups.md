# Gate 3 Mockups Template

## Outputs

- `.opencode/docs/design/mockups.md`
- Mockup assets or links, if applicable.
- `.opencode/design-handoff/context.md` approval notes when external designs are the source.
- `.opencode/project-status.md`

## Exit Criteria

- Desktop, tablet, and mobile design expectations are approved or marked not applicable.
- Visual, responsive, and accessibility concerns are documented.
- External design context and supporting files in `.opencode/design-handoff/` have an approval status and implementation guidance.
- Allowed deviations from the external design are documented before implementation.
- For prepared designs, exact-match requirements and any approved deviations are documented screen by screen or component by component.
- Technology expectations from the handoff are approved for implementation or each deviation has a recorded rationale and approval.
- `.opencode/project-status.md` shows approval status, blockers, and the recommended next action.

## External Design Handoff Checklist

- Compare supplied screenshots/assets/prototypes/exports with `.opencode/design-handoff/context.md` for consistency.
- Record breakpoints, layout rules, typography, colors, spacing, and components.
- Record required states: default, hover, focus, pressed, loading, empty, error, disabled.
- Confirm accessibility expectations: contrast, keyboard focus, labels, reduced motion, and touch targets.
- Decide what must match exactly and what may be adapted to the app framework.
- Confirm how design assets, exported HTML/CSS, icon sets, fonts, imagery, motion, and named technologies should be used in the production stack.
