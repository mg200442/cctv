# Gate 7 Review Template

## Exit Criteria

- Code, tests, docs, security, accessibility, UX, and release risks are reviewed.
- Blockers and follow-ups are recorded in `.opencode/project-status.md`; long-form backlog detail may go in `.opencode/tasks/backlog.md`.
- Release readiness is stated clearly.
- Final report exists under `.opencode/reports/`.
- `.opencode/project-status.md` shows release readiness and remaining next actions.
